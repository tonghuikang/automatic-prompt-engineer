- **Leading/Loaded:** Questions that suggest a particular answer or contain biased terms.
- **Ambiguity:** Unclear or overly complex phrasing that makes the intent difficult to discern.
- **Off-Topic:** Questions that do not align with the expected subject matter or are irrelevant.
- **Assumptions:** Questions that include unstated premises or biases.
- **Implicit Assumptions:** Questions that contain hidden or indirect premises that may not be universally accepted.
- **Insufficient Context:** Questions that rely heavily on specific contexts or niche topics without providing adequate background information.
- **Loaded Language:** Use of emotionally charged or biased terms that influence the respondent's perspective.
